Reflective materials

  • Specular materials reflect incoming rays perfectly around the surface normal

  • Diffuse materials reflect incoming rays towards a random point on a unit sphere placed with its base on the contact point

  • Glossy materials are in-between, reflecting incoming rays in a random 'oval' shape biased towards the direction of a specular reflection.
